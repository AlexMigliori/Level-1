Breakfast Burrito

  • Previous Recipe
  • Next Recipe
Yields 4Prep time:25 minutesCook time:20 minutes

The breakfast burrito has lots of flavor and kick, perfect start to long day of work.

Ingredients:
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • Pinch of salt and pepper
  • 1 Tbsp Cholula hot sauce, or other hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup Shredded cheese. It can be cheddar or pepper jack or really any shredded cheese that you prefer.
  • 1 bell pepper diced.
  • 1 jalapeno diced.
  • 1/2 cup Italian sausage. You can use other sausage but i like the kick of italian sausage.
  • 2 large flour tortillas.
Instructions:
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk together.
  2. Add the shredded cheese to the bowl and whisk into the eggs.
  3. Dice the bell pepper and jalapeno.
  4. Put the peppers and sausage in a skillet to cook
  5. Add some butter to a saucepan. Add the eggs to a saucepan
  6. Once the peppers and sausage is cooked add to the eggs.
  7. Stir the eggs constantly while it cooks on low to medium heat.
  8. Remove from heat once eggs are cooked
  9. Put tortilla on skillet to warm up, add some cheese if you desire.
  10. Remove tortilla and add eggs to the middle of the tortilla. Wrap tortilla up in a burrito.
  11. Add burrito back to skillet to sear the top and bottom
  12. Serve with salsa or any other topping you desire.
