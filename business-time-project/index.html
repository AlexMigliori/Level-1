Buffalo Chicken Fries

  • Previous Recipe
  • Next Recipe
Yield 4-6prep time:10 minutescook time:25 minutes

This is the best buffalo chicken fries you will ever eat. Everything about it is so yummy and tasty, which is why i want to share with you.

Ingredients:
  • A couple peeled potatoes. You can use frozen french fries, but fresh cut fries are just better.
  • One and a half chicken breasts cooked and cut into small cuts or strips.
  • Frank's hot sauce. Other buffalo sauces may work but Frank's is the best for this.
  • Shredded cheese. It can be cheddar or pepper jack or really any shredded cheese that you prefer.
  • Blue cheese or some crumbly cheese. I like blue cheese, so it is my go to for this.
  • Ranch dressing.
  • Green onions for topping.
Instructions:
  1. Cut potatoes into french fries, as evenly as possible.
  2. Soak the potatoes in cold water for about 30 minutes. Add a little salt to the bowl as well.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  4. Spread french fries onto a sheet pan and cover with oil and salt.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes. Then turn oven up to 425 and bake for another 20 to reach that golden color.
  6. Chop the chicken into bite size pieces or strips, then coat them in buffalo sauce.
  7. Remove fries from oven and sprinkle half the shredded cheese and the chicken pieces.
  8. Return pan to oven and cook for another 6 minutes.
  9. Remove and cover fries with buffalo sauce and ranch dressing.
  10. Finish it off with crumbled blue cheese pieces and green onions. Serve immediately.
