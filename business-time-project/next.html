Oven-baked Lemon salmon

  • Previous Recipe
  • Next Recipe
Yields 6Prep time:10 minutesCook time:15 minutes

This lemon salmon melts in your mouth, it has my wife asing for it at least once a week.

Ingredients:
  • 1/3 cup butter.
  • 1 lemon.
  • 1 tbsp of minced garlic.
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar.
  • Salt and pepper.
  • 2 salmon filets.
  • Parsley for topping.
Instructions:
  1. Turn oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with foil and place salmon on top.
  3. Squeeze the lemon onto each filet.
  4. In a small sauce pan, melt the butter and add the garlic to it.
  5. Once melted add the brown sugar and remove from heat, stir till smooth.
  6. Cover the salmon with the sauce.
  7. Sprinkle salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Cook salmon for 8-10 minutes depending on size of filets.
  9. Remove and garnish with parsley, serve with rice or couscous.
